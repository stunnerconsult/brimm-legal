Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my Brimm Pro subscription?
Open the iOS Settings app → tap your name at the top → tap Subscriptions → Brimm → Cancel Subscription. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.

How does the hydration weighting work?
Brimm uses common hydration science to count drinks based on how they actually hydrate you. Water counts at 100%, juice at 85%, soda at 70%, and coffee at 60%. So a 250 ml coffee contributes 150 ml toward your daily goal.

Where is my data stored?
All your data lives locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We don't have servers and we never see your data. If you enable Apple Health sync, your drinks are written to the Health app on your device.

How do I change my daily goal?
Open the Settings tab inside Brimm → use the Daily Goal slider to set your target between 500-4,000 ml.

Can I delete a logged drink?
Yes. On the Today screen, long-press any drink in your list and choose Delete.

My reminders aren't working — what should I check?
Open iOS Settings → Brimm → Notifications, and make sure notifications are allowed. Inside Brimm, go to Settings → Reminders and confirm the toggle is on. Use the "Send Test Reminder" button to verify they're firing correctly.
