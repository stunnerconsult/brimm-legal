Privacy Policy

Effective June 2026

Our Promise

Brimm is built for privacy. All your data stays on your device. We don't have servers, we don't run analytics, and we don't share anything with third parties.

What We Store

Your drink logs, daily goals, and reminder times are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your phone unless you choose to sync it with Apple Health.

Apple Health

If you grant permission, Brimm writes drink data to Apple Health as dietary water. This data stays within Apple's secure HealthKit system. You can revoke access anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health.

Notifications

Reminder notifications are scheduled entirely on your device. Brimm does not send push notifications from any server.

Subscriptions

Brimm Pro subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment information. You can manage or cancel anytime in iOS Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions.

Tracking

Brimm uses no analytics, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs. We do not track you across apps or websites.

Children

Brimm is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children.

Your Rights

Delete the Brimm app to remove all local data. Use the Apple Health app to manage data synced there.
